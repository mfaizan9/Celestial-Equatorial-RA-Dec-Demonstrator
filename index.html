The Celestial Sphere

Drag anywhere on the sphere to rotate your view of it. Drag the star (on the near side) to move it, or use the Star Position sliders. Click the diagram (or Tab to it) to give it keyboard focus, then use the arrow keys; press Enter to switch the arrow keys between rotating the view and moving the star (Shift + arrows for larger steps). Clicking the star or the sphere also sets which one the arrows control.
