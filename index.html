The Celestial Sphere

Drag anywhere on the sphere to rotate your view of it, or drag the star (on the near side) to move it. For keyboard control, Tab to the diagram and use the arrow keys to rotate the view; Tab again to select the star and use the arrow keys to move it (Shift for larger / finer steps). The Star Position sliders also move the star.
